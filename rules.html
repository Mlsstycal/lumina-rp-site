Server Rules

These rules exist to protect the quality of roleplay for every player in the city. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. By connecting to Lumina RP, you agree to follow them.

Core Rules

01 Respect the Roleplay Environment ›

Stay in character at all times. No trolling, metagaming, or powergaming. The quality of your roleplay directly affects the experience of every other player around you. Take it seriously.

02 Be Respectful to Others ›

Treat all players and staff with respect — both in-character and out-of-character. Harassment, hate speech, and targeted toxicity will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

03 No Random Deathmatch (RDM) ›

You must have a valid in-character reason before attacking or killing another player. Killing someone without proper RP escalation is RDM and will result in punishment.

04 No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) ›

Using vehicles as weapons without a valid RP reason is not allowed. Ramming, running players over, or using cars as offensive tools without proper escalation will be treated the same as RDM.

05 Follow the Chain of Command ›

Listen to admins and moderators. If you are told to stop or correct a behavior, do so immediately. Disputes can always be handled through proper channels — not in-game arguments.

06 No Cheating or Exploiting ›

Any use of mods, exploits, or third-party programs to gain an unfair advantage will result in a permanent ban. This includes economy exploits, duplication glitches, or any form of server manipulation.

07 Don't Fail RP ›

If you are caught breaking immersion — joking OOC mid-scene, not valuing your life, breaking character during serious RP — you may be warned, kicked, or banned depending on severity and history.

Punishment Guidelines

Enforcement Guide

Verbal Warning
First-time minor offenses. Staff will address the issue in-game or via Discord DM.
Kick
Repeated minor offenses or moderate violations that disrupt active RP.
Temp Ban
RDM, VDM, fail RP, harassment, or any offense that significantly affects other players. Duration at staff discretion.
Permanent Ban
Cheating, exploiting, or repeated serious violations. Permanent bans are reviewed on appeal only.
Appeals
All ban appeals must be submitted through the official Discord. Staff decisions are final unless overturned on appeal.

Staff Decisions Are Final

These rules are designed to protect the quality of RP for everyone. If you have questions or concerns, reach out to staff on Discord — not in-game. We're here to help, but we will enforce consistently.
