Getting Started — Clocking In

Before you can use any police commands or appear on dispatch you must clock in. You have two options:

Option 1 — Duty Sign: Head to the duty sign inside Mission Row PD and press E to sign on duty.
Option 2 — F5 Job Menu: Press F5 to open your job menu and toggle on duty from there.

Once on duty you will appear on the map for other officers and dispatch will track your location in real time.

MDT — Mobile Data Terminal

Type /openmdt to open the MDT. This is your primary investigative and record-keeping tool.

TabWhat you can do
CitizensSearch players by name or citizenid. View full profiles including mugshots, fingerprints, registered vehicles, houses. Manage licenses. Add tags to flag known criminals.
IncidentsCreate incident reports for any situation. Link suspects, vehicles, and evidence. Add officer notes. All saved permanently to the database.
BOLOsCreate Be On Lookout alerts for suspects or vehicles. Visible to all on duty officers. Remove once the situation is resolved.
DispatchView all active calls and their locations. See which officers are responding. Track officer locations on the map in real time.

Evidence Collection Workflow

When guns are fired shell casings automatically appear on the ground. When players are shot blood drops appear. Officers collect these as evidence using empty evidence bags from the evidence supply room.

#Action
1Arrive at crime scene and secure the area
2Look for blood drops and shell casings on the ground
3Equip your empty evidence bag from inventory or the evidence supply room
4Walk up to evidence and press E to collect it
5Bag becomes a filled evidence bag with DNA or ballistic info attached
6Return to station and store bags in the evidence locker
7Note the DNA hash from the bag info for your incident report
8Search MDT for matching fingerprints or DNA records
9Create an incident report linking all evidence to suspects
//

Arresting & Booking a Suspect

Follow this exact workflow every time you make an arrest. Every step must be completed for a valid booking.

#ActionHow
1Detain Suspect/cuff or /sc — or use radial menu Player Actions
2Search SuspectRadial menu → Police → Search Player
3Escort to Station/escort or radial menu → Player Actions → Escort
4FingerprintWalk suspect to fingerprint scanner inside station → press E
5MugshotWalk suspect to mugshot camera inside station → press E
6DNA Sample/takedna [serverid] while holding an empty evidence bag
7MDT Report/openmdt → Citizens → find suspect → create incident report
8JailRadial menu → Police → Jail Player → enter time in months
9Fine (if applicable)Radial menu → Police → Bill Player → enter fine amount

Use of Force Guidelines

LevelTypeWhen to use
1Verbal CommandsAlways attempt verbal first before any physical action
2Soft RestraintUse /sc for compliant or cooperative suspects
3Hard RestraintUse /cuff for resistive or dangerous suspects
4Lethal ForceOnly when life is in immediate danger — always document in MDT incident report

Do's and Don'ts

✓ Always
  • Clock in before starting patrol
  • Document all arrests in the MDT with a full incident report
  • Take fingerprints and mugshot when booking a suspect
  • Store all collected evidence in the evidence locker
  • Use proper radio channels for all communications
  • Follow use of force guidelines at all times
  • Treat all players with respect
✗ Never
  • Abuse police commands or your authority
  • Jail someone without a completed MDT report
  • Impound a vehicle without valid RP justification
  • Use /fine without proper roleplay basis
  • Share MDT information out of character
  • Use lethal force without exhausting other options
  • Act on information you would not know in character
//
