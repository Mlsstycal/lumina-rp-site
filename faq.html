// Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before jumping into the city. If you can't find your answer here, hop in our Discord and a staff member will help you out.

Getting Started

How do I join Lumina RP? +

Open FiveM and use the direct connect link: cfx.re/join/89ej3m. You can also search "Lumina RP" in the FiveM server browser. Make sure you've joined our Discord first — important announcements, updates, and whitelisting happen there.

Is Lumina RP whitelisted? +

The server is open to all players for general roleplay. However, certain roles — such as Police, EMS, Gang Leader, and Business Owner — require an approved application before you can access them. Check the relevant pages on this site to apply.

Do I need any experience to play? +

No prior FiveM experience is required. However, we do expect all players to understand and follow our server rules before connecting. New players are welcome — just come ready to roleplay seriously and respect others in the city.

What is the minimum age requirement? +

You must be 18 or older to apply for any whitelisted role (Police, EMS, Gang, Business, or Staff). For general server access there is no enforced age minimum, but maturity and proper roleplay behavior are expected from all players at all times.

What makes Lumina RP different from other servers? +

Lumina RP runs 1,000+ custom clothing items, 100+ custom scripts, a full skill tree system, custom drug labs, gang territory mechanics, missions, and staff-run city events. Everything is built to reward active, quality roleplay — not just time spent online. The city is alive, and your choices actually shape it.

Rules & Conduct

What is RDM and VDM? +

RDM (Random Deathmatch) is attacking or killing another player without a valid in-character reason or proper RP escalation. VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) is using a vehicle as a weapon without RP justification. Both are serious violations and can result in a ban. Always escalate through roleplay before any violent action.

What is metagaming? +

Metagaming is using information your character wouldn't realistically know — for example, acting on something you saw in a stream, heard in a Discord call, or read in OOC chat. Your character can only know what they've been told or experienced in-game. Metagaming breaks immersion for everyone and is a bannable offense.

What is powergaming? +

Powergaming is forcing outcomes on other players without giving them a fair chance to respond — for example, performing actions that would be physically impossible, or ignoring someone's reaction to your roleplay. Good RP is collaborative. Give others the opportunity to respond and shape the story with you.

What happens if I break a rule? +

Depending on the severity, consequences range from a verbal warning to a temporary or permanent ban. First-time minor offenses typically result in a warning. Serious violations like cheating or exploiting result in a permanent ban. You can find the full punishment guide on our Rules page. All bans can be appealed through Discord.

Can I be a criminal and a legal character at the same time? +

Yes — and it makes for some of the best RP on the server. You can own a business, hold a legal front, and still operate in the underground. Just understand that actions have consequences. If your name gets out as a criminal, expect police attention, tax hikes from the government, or your business becoming a target. Play smart.

Applications & Roles

How long does it take to hear back on an application? +

Most applications are reviewed within 24–48 hours. Dynasty and senior whitelisted roles may take longer due to the selective nature of the process. You'll hear back via Discord DM from a staff member. Make sure your DMs are open and you're in the Lumina Discord.

Can I apply for multiple roles at once? +

You can submit multiple applications, but certain roles may conflict with each other. For example, an active gang member may not be eligible for a Police role simultaneously. Staff will discuss any conflicts during the review process. When in doubt, ask in Discord before applying.

My application was denied — can I reapply? +

Yes. If your application is denied, staff will typically explain why. Take the feedback seriously, improve where needed, and reapply after a reasonable waiting period. Low-effort applications that are resubmitted without any changes will be denied again.

What is a Dynasty role? +

Dynasty roles are high-impact, story-driven positions in the city — think city leadership, real estate power players, or figures who shape long-term server storylines. They require significant commitment and are selectively approved. If you're a serious, story-driven roleplayer who wants to leave a lasting mark on the city, this is for you. Apply here.

Server & Technical

Is the server up 24/7? +

Yes, Lumina RP runs 24/7. Scheduled maintenance or restarts will be announced in the Discord server ahead of time. If the server is unexpectedly down, check the Discord for updates before submitting a support ticket.

I'm having a technical issue in-game. Who do I contact? +

Open a support ticket in the Discord server. Describe your issue clearly — include your in-game name, what happened, and any steps you've already tried. Do not DM staff directly for technical issues as tickets ensure the right person sees your request.

Do donations give an unfair advantage? +

No. Donations through our Tebex store go directly toward server costs, development, and improvements. Lumina RP is built on the principle that your progress is earned through roleplay, not purchased. Every contribution is appreciated but never required to play or advance in the city.

I was banned. How do I appeal? +

All ban appeals must be submitted through the official Discord server using the proper appeal channel. Be honest, be respectful, and explain your situation clearly. Appeals are reviewed by senior staff and decisions are final unless new information comes to light.

Still have a question?

Can't find what you're looking for? Our staff and community are active in the Discord daily. Drop a message and someone will get back to you.
