Gang System — Los Santos Underground

BUILD YOUR EMPIRE

Lumina RP runs a structured 3-tier gang system built for serious roleplay. Every decision matters, every street has a story. Claim your territory. Rise through the ranks.

Gang Structure

Size Requirements

  • Minimum: 3–5 members
  • Maximum: 15 members
  • All members must be active
  • High-quality RP required
️

Combat Rules

  • 5-man rule in all engagements
  • 15-min cooldown between conflicts
  • All conflict must be RP-driven
  • Proper escalation required
️

Territory

  • Each gang may claim a home block
  • All members may defend territory
  • 5-man rule applies to turf wars
  • Roleplay escalation required
⏳

Switching & Cooldowns

  • Cooldown before joining new gang
  • Cannot fight for new gang immediately
  • XP-based tier progression
  • Gang tablets for upgrades

Strike System

  • 3 strikes = gang disbanded
  • Strikes: poor RP, rule violations
  • Strike duration: 1–2 months
  • Staff decisions are final

Gang Upgrades

  • Larger stash sizes via tablet
  • CCTV systems
  • Gang-exclusive vehicles
  • Custom crafting tables (Tier 1)

Quick Rules Reference

MAX IN COMBAT
5 members per gang in any active conflict or shootout
WAR COOLDOWN
15 minutes minimum before re-engagement after a conflict ends
GANG SIZE
Minimum 3–5 members · Maximum 15 members
STRIKES
3 strikes results in gang disbandment · Duration: 1–2 months or staff discretion
GANG SWITCH
Cooldown period required · Cannot immediately participate in conflict on another gang's side
DISCORD
All approved gangs receive a private text + voice channel in the Lumina Gang Discord
