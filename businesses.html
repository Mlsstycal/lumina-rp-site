Ownership Rules & Expectations

General Expectations

Be active and present within your business. Create high-quality, engaging roleplay for the community. Maintain a professional environment and be open to working with staff on setup and improvements.

Business Responsibilities

Keep your business operational and staffed when possible. Hire and manage employees fairly. Establish a clear theme, menu, and identity. Host events or activities to keep your business active.

Inactivity Policy

Extended inactivity without notice may result in your business being reassigned. If you need time away, communicate with staff ahead of time — we're flexible if you're upfront.

Abuse & Misuse

No abusing your business for unfair advantages or exploiting systems. No breaking server rules under the protection of ownership. Staff reserve the right to intervene if necessary.

Illegal Activity & Roleplay

Running a business doesn't mean staying clean. Engaging in illegal activity will not result in losing your business — in fact, it opens the door for deeper roleplay. However, actions have consequences. If your name gets out, expect police attention, potential blackmail, and raised taxes.

Final Note

We want business owners here to build, create, and lead. Your business isn't just a location — it's an experience for the entire city. Play it smart. What you do behind the scenes can shape your entire story.
